A set of documents, submitted to journal editorial and organization of reviewing

The set of documents submitted to the editorial board of the journal

The author, who represents the manuscript for publication, together with copies of the article, which are printed and signed by all co-authors to the editorial office submits:

  1. Statement (in the presence of co-authors – joint and signed by all co-authors) that the article is the author's own development (authors), has never been published anywhere else and is not being considered in other editions. The authors give written consent to the publication of the material in the edition of the National Transport University.
  2. An extract from the protocol of the department, laboratory or scientific unit that recommends the article for publication.
  3. Reference about the author (authors) (photo of author, surname, name, degree, academic rank, honorary title, Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID), place of work, position, postal address for correspondence, contact number, e -mail).
  4. Certificate of Consent for the processing of personal data (for each author).
  5. An external review of an article compiled by a specialist in this area of research, a doctor of sciences (a scientist, a head of a public administration or a structural unit of a state administration connected with a research direction, the head of an enterprise or an organization related to the field of research) that is not an employee of NTU. The review should be sealed with the seal of the institution, enterprise, organization. Information about reviewers should be made in the same way as information about the authors of the article and placed in the journal after the information about the authors.

Requirements for external review

Publication and authorship

  1. The name of the publication, the names and surnames of the authors, indicating the scientific degrees, titles and positions (if any), shuld be indicated.
  2. The general character of scientific work should be shown: experimental, theoretical, review, description of the new methodology or technical system, short message; the result of work in accordance with the set goals and objectives.
  3. The relevance of the list of used literature shoul be noted.
  4. The profile of the article should be specifiesed.

The character of the publication

  1. The style of presentation of the article's material: clear, compressed, requires reduction, processing, unreasonably many formulas, figures, contains new data of analysis, experiment, theory, does not contain essential scientific results, has practical value, needs to be supplemented and refined.
  2. Scientific content of the article: original, previously not published in full.
  3. Observations and recommendations should be given.
  4. Conclusion on publication should be given.

Information about the reviewer

  1. Surname, initials, indicating the scientific degree, rank, position and Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID) (it can be obtained through free registration on the site https://orcid.org/register).
  2. Place of work and contact details.

Internal review

All manuscripts are initially considered by the editors to assess their relevance to the subject and requirements of the journal.

Internal (single blind) review is carried out only if there is a complete set of documents prepared appropriately, executed in accordance with the requirements for the article and the accompanying documents.

The requirements and ethical obligations of reviewers are given in the section «The Ethical Code of reviewers»

Internal review procedure

  1. The manuscript is transmitted to the reviewer - a representative of the editorial board, who is a specialist in this area of research.
  2. The reviewer evaluates the manuscript style and presentation of the material, scientific content, compliance with the requirements of registration, verifies the manuscript for plagiarism. The reviewer provides comments, recommendations and opinion on the possibility of publishing.

Procedure for re-examination

When receiving a refusal to publish the article (according to the reviewer's opinion), the author has the right to revision, rectify and re-submit the manuscript for review. In the cover letter to the editorial board, it is necessary to indicate changes which were made in accordance with the recommendations of the reviewer or to justify autors own statement in case of disagreement with the conclusions of the reviewer.
